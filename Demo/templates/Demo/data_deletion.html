Data Deletion Instructions

Follow these steps to request deletion of your data from Zid Analytics.

Step 1: Identify Your Account

Log in to your Zid.sa account and confirm your store or user details.

Step 2: Submit Deletion Request

Send an email to support@zid.sa with:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address used with Zid
  • Your store ID (if applicable)
  • A brief statement requesting data deletion
Step 3: Confirmation

We will review your request and respond within 7 business days. Once completed, you will receive confirmation that your data has been permanently deleted from our systems.

Important Notes
  • Deleted data cannot be recovered.
  • Some anonymized analytics data may remain for system integrity, but will not contain personal identifiers.
